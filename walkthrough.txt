Walkthrough of the text adventure game 'The Overlook'

The Pantry room:
Pick up the fireaxe and use it on the door. Go north.

The Kitchen:
Enter the diner to the west, get the money. Go east twice to enter the bar.
Use the money or talk to loyd and buy a drink. Speak with him again and 
he will drop his keycard. Get the keycard. Now go west to the kitchen
and use the keycard. The lobby door to the north will open. Go north without the knife.

The Lobby:
Talk to Wendy, go south to the kitchen. Use the knife. Good Job!
